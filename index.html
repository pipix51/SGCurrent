This is the main page for the SGCurrent application. This application was created by Y3 DHSJHICC members.

You can choose to browse the articles about national education, public policies and current affairs which we offer. We hope that you, the user, would be able to gain some knowledge through using this app. We strongly encourage you to post comments on topics and articles you have an opinion on. You can even send an article you wish to be published in the app (just look in the Administrators tab).

As this app is still a work-in-progress, we hope that you would be able to provide us with your opinions of this app. If you have a query, look in the Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) tab to check if your question exists before contacting the administrators. If you have discovered any bugs, you can either contact the administrators or preferably leave a comment on the Google Play Store
