Summary

Asia is by far, the world's biggest and most populated continent, being home to countries such as India and China (both countries with populations of more than 1 billion people each). Asia stretches from its western border with Europe at Turkey to its eastern border with the North American continent; covering from the North Pole (at Russia) to the border with Australia. Many sub-regions exist in Asia, such as East Asia, Southeast Asia, North Asia, Central Asia and the Middle East. Many different races and religions flourish in Asia, ranging from the Chinese, to Arabs and Indians, and to the Malays and the Japanese.
