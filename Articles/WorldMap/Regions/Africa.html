Summary

Although most of the Africa population remain poor, it is home to a lot of natural miracles and resources, such as wildlife and precious minerals. It is also believed as where human beings first originate, with most of the oldest fossils found in East Africa. One of the largest ancient civilisation, the Egyptian Civilisation, is also located in Africa. With globalisation, Africa is also rising, producing supercities that control the world to some extent. However, there are some problems that we must address, such as poaching, civil wars and lack of infrastructure.
