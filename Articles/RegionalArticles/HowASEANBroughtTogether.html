Firstly, what is ASEAN? ASEAN, The Association of Southeast Asian Nations is a regional group that promotes economic, political, and security cooperation among its ten members

ASEAN consists of 10 states - Brunei Darussalum, Indonesia, Laos, Cambodia, Singapore, Thailand, Vietnam, Malaysia, Myanmar and Philippines

Opinions Of Singapore Teenagers

The Government of Singapore has also recognised the powerful and far-reaching benefits which socially aware teenagers can bring. A good example of this can be found in Youth Corps Singapore, which was founded by the Ministry of Culture, Community and Youth. The goal of Youth Corps Singapre, to quote from the official website [3], is to-

Bringing together Singapore Citizens and PRs aged 16 to 35, Youth Corps Singapore provides a platform for youths to learn new skills and meet like-minded peers, who aspire to go forth and create a better Singapore and a better world for all.

To conclude, social awareness in teenagers is important in teenagers; Singaporean teenagers should have social awareness in order for society to benefit as a whole in the long term. Not only will social awareness increase the knowledge of the affected and underprivileged in our society, it will also increase the chances of actions being taken to ensure that all Singaporeans can begin on an equal footing, thus giving rise to a truly meritocratic society.
