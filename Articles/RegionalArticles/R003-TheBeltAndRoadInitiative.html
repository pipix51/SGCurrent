The Belt And Road Initiative

Introduction

The Belt and Road Initiative(B&R), also known as the Silk Road Economic Belt and 21th Century Maritime Silk Road, is developed by China. It focus on connecting Eurasian countries, and also the cooperation between them, expescially with the People's Republic of China.

Started in 2013, this strategy aims to push China to participate in larger global affairs. Through boosting the infrastructure in Asian countries and accelerating economic growth in Asia and Europe. Due to various reasons, many countries expressed their interest in joining.

This initiative plans to promote cooperation between countries such that all the countries can attain a win-win situation and mutual benefits; meanwhile establishing peace and stability. Since the start of this initiative, China has cooperated with many countries.

One important project is the Madaraka Express in Kenya, connecting Mombasa and Nairobi. Opened to the public on 31st May 2017, it is the first railway in Kenya and one of the few in the Africa Continent.

China is also currently building highways in Pakistan; railways in Indonesia, Iran, Laos; and powerstations in Bangladesh.

Half of the world is connected by the R&B. It definitely will benefit the world in the long run.
